    My Itinerary

    Vistited Cities

    My first trip was to Alaska. Flying from Los Angeles, CA to Seattle, WA. Where I was left short for words with sights of nature I had never encountered. A seven-day cruise including destinations like: Seattle, Juneau, Skagway, Ketchikan, Victoria, Vancouver.

    Later I was sent on my Second Assignment aboard a vessel in Europe. Having flown from Mexico City to Munich, Germany. I finally boarded my ship in Dubrovnik, Croatia. I was truly astonished with all the beauty this country had to offer. It was a true feeling of being in Europe. Hitting ports like: Greece, Spain, France, Monaco, Malta, United Kingdom, Italy, Monte Negro, Croatia, Sicily and Turkey.

    Third Journey was when I decided to move to Aguascalientes, Mexico for a few years. This is where I visited beautiful states like: Tamaulipas, Zacatecas, San Luis, Jalisco, Michoacan, Baja California Sur, Mexico City, Quintana Roo, Ixtapa, Guanajuato, Oaxaca, Guadalajara, and Zacatecas.
