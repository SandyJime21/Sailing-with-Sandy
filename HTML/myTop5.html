    My Top 5

    Sights

    Bora Bora, French Polynesia

    Bora Bora is mystical paradise filled with beauty and wonders. This Beauty is the place to go if you’re looking for a breath-taking adventure. With crystal blue water, so clear you can see the sea creatures such as: Sting Rays, White Tip Shark, Sea Turtle swimming under your boat.

    Loyalty Islands, Coral Sea

    Loyalty Islands is where the surrounding waters are known for their natural beauty, great biodiversity, and unique ecological processes. They possess healthy marine life with many species of fish and corals, a reef system that is among the world’s most extensive, and one of the largest and most beautiful lagoons on the planet.

    Tauranga, New Zeland

    Tauranga, New Zeland is where you find great cafes, small boutiques, and with coast on one side and beautiful, lush green mountain on the other. With Antarctic breezes hitting south of this island, this is where you find the vivid blues and pinks skys. This is, in my opinion, truly the beauty of New Zealand.





    Kodiak, Alaska

    Kodiak, Alaska is a true wonder. You find Humpbacks Whales breaching and playing alongside their newborn calves. Bald Eagles sweeping down to snatch jumping Salmon from the sea. Deer running thru the green forest, and occasional black bear sightings. If you want to take a deep breath of fresh air this is where to do it. Every turn you make is filled with natures true beauty and wonders.

    Moorea Island, Tahiti

    Moorea Island, Tahiti is a South Pacific island, part of French Polynesia's Society Islands archipelago. It's known for its jagged volcanic mountains and sandy beaches. Nothing short of a true mystic beauty.
    Architecture

    Sydney, Australia

    Sydney, Australia is know for Sydney Opera House. Located on the banks of Sydney Harbour, it is regarded as one of the world’s most famous buildings. Designed by Danish architect Jørn Utzon, but completed by an Australian architectural team headed by Peter Hall, the building was formally opened on 20 October 1973

    Naples, Italy

    Naples, Italy is home to Castel Nuovo ("New Castle "). A medieval castel located in front of the Piazza Municipio ("City Hall "). Dating 1279. Was the royal seat for kings of Naples. Its overwhelming size is sure to make your jaw drop.
    Now used as the headquarters of Neapolitan Society of Homeland History.

    Palma de Mallorca, Spain

    Palma de Mallorca, Spain where you find The Royal Palace of La Almudaina. The Palace is said to be built around the 14th century.
    It was the royal seat to King Philip II of Spain.
    Its decoration presents a medieval style with decorations from 15th-20th centuries.

    Istanbul, Turkey

    Istanbul, Turkey is home to the Blue Mosque. It was constructed from 1609 to 1616, during the rule of Sultan Ahmed I, who decided to have it built after war with Persia. It contains Sultan Ahmed's tomb.
    Designed with hand-painted blue tiles that adorn the mosque’s interior walls, blue lights that frame the mosque’s five main domes, six minarets and eight secondary domes.

    Pisa, Italy

    Pisa, Italy is where you find the Leaning Tower of Pisa. The tower is situated behind the Pisa Cathedral. The tower began to lean during construction in the 12th century, due to soft ground which could not properly support the structure's weight, and it worsened through the completion of construction in the 14th century.
    Walks

    Santorini, Greece

    Santorini, Greece is nothing short or a dream. Every corner has the blues and white you dream about in movies. As you walk into this magical city, you are met with Gyro aromas from every direction. If you choose to walk down, you will will see Greek mules at every corver, these truly are giant creatures.

    Victoria, Canada

    Victoria, Canada is located on Southern tip of Vancouver Island.
    This colorful city was named after Queen Victoria of England. Known as Garden city, for its ever-blooming gardens on every corner. This City is a true delight to the eyes, as Garden City colors meet the Blue of the Pacific.

    Gibraltar, United Kingdom

    Gibraltar, United Kingdom is a British Overseas Territory located at the Southern tip of the Iberian Peninsula. The Rock of Gibraltar is a walk to take, home to the Barbary Macaques Monkey. These little thieves are sure to take anything shinny that grabs their attention. On this walk, you will find color changing caves. At the tip of the Rock, you can oversea the Northern African tip, the South-West terrain of Spain, and have an overlook of the Gibraltar Bay.

    Venice, Italy

    Venice, Italy is a walk of a life time. From San Marcos Square to crossing the Venezia Gran Canal. This small city is pure magic, that you won’t soon forget. The best way to discover this little wonder is by foot. With all the canals and similar buildings, make sure to take a map as it can become easy to lose you path back.

    Juneau, Alaska

    Juneau, Alaska is a small town, filled with natural wonder. It is at the foot of the Alaskan forest. With occasional showers and Bald Eagles soaring above you, it is an experience to be had by nature lovers.
